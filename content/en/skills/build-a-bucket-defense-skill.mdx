Allocating your points correctly is the most vital step in establishing a powerful **build a bucket defense skill**. In the current 2026 season, the "All-Around" defender is often less effective than a "Specialized Lockdown." You must decide whether you want to protect the interior or lock down the perimeter. Physical profile attributes also play a massive role. You cannot ignore **Strength** or **Speed**. Strength allows you to hold your ground against larger slashers, while Speed ensures you aren't left behind during fast breaks.

  <Step num="1" title="Initial Physical Foundation">

  <Step num="3" title="Unlocking Tier 1 Badges">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Interior & Block Reinforcement">
    Once your perimeter game is stable, invest in **Block** and **Strength**. This ensures that if a guard does get past you, you have the verticality to recover with a "chase-down" block or a strong interior contest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Optimization">
    Max out your primary stats and focus on **Stamina**. In the fourth quarter, defensive stats effectively drop if your stamina is low. High stamina keeps your defensive ratings "active" longer.
  </Step>
