<Section icon={Target} title="Optimizing Wingspan, Height, and Badges">

Physical dimensions dictate your attribute ceilings. A longer wingspan improves defense and finishing but penalizes your shooting. Understanding these trade-offs is essential to fine-tuning your setups and ensuring you do not waste valuable attribute points.

When adjusting wingspan, look for the sweet spot where you maximize defensive reach without dropping your primary shooting or ball-handling stats below key badge thresholds.

| Wingspan Length | Three-Point Cap | Driving Dunk Cap | Steal Cap | Best For |
