<Section icon={Map} title="Core Mechanics of the Build a Bucket Rating Breakdown">

Understanding the **build a bucket rating breakdown** is the first step toward moving from a casual player to a master builder. Build-A-Bucket is a browser-based basketball simulation where players spin a wheel of NBA stars and extract a single skill from each result. The challenge lies in the fact that you only have nine slots to fill, and once a category is occupied, that decision is permanent for the duration of the run.

The game evaluates your custom player based on nine distinct categories: Finishing, Mid-range, Threes, Passing, Ball Handling, Rebounding, Post D, Perimeter D, and Athleticism. To achieve a high overall rating (OVR), you must avoid "dead slots"—categories where an unsuitable player produces a C or D grade. The rating system is sensitive; even a build with eight A+ categories can be dragged down to a 94 or 95 OVR if the final slot is a failing grade.
