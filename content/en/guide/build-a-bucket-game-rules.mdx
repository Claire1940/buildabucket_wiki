The **build a bucket game rules** require you to complete exactly nine categories. Each category contributes to your final Overall Rating (OVR) and determines how your player performs during the season simulation. | Attribute | Primary Role | Ideal Player Profile | Impact on Simulation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Mid-range** | Pull-up shooting | Technical shot creators | Essential for scoring consistency |
| **Threes** | Perimeter spacing | Volume shooters (Curry/Thompson) | Boosts offensive efficiency |
