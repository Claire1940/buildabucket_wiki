<Section icon={Map} title="Choosing Your Starting Position">

Selecting the right position is the foundational step in the player creation process. Your position determines your base attribute caps, physical limits, and the types of badges your character can unlock as they level up. Beginners should choose a position that matches their preferred playstyle on the court.

| Position | Primary Role | Key Attributes | Recommended Height |
| PG | Playmaking and speed | Passing, Ball Handle | 6'1" to 6'3" |
| SG | Perimeter scoring | Three-Point, Mid-Range | 6'4" to 6'6" |
| SF | All-around utility | Defending, Finishing | 6'7" to 6'9" |
| PF | Paint protection and rebounding | Rebounding, Strength | 6'9" to 6'11" |
| C | Inside dominance | Block, Interior Defense | 7'0" to 7'2" |

New players often struggle with extreme builds. Creating a player that is too short limits defensive capabilities, while creating a player that is too tall significantly reduces movement speed. Finding a balanced physical profile ensures you can contribute on both ends of the court.

<Section icon={Target} title="Stat Distribution and Attribute Caps">

Allocating attribute points determines how your player performs during key game scenarios. Points should be distributed to hit specific thresholds that unlock animations and badges. Spreading points too thin across all categories results in a mediocre player who cannot excel at any single task.

| Attribute Category | Key Sub-Stats | Gameplay Impact | Threshold Target |
| Finishing | Layup, Dunk, Close Shot | Scoring inside the key | 85 for Contact Dunks |
| Shooting | Mid-Range, Three-Point | Perimeter scoring efficiency | 80 for Silver Badges |
| Playmaking | Pass Accuracy, Ball Handle | Ball security and passing speed | 75 for Basic Dribble Packages |
| Defense | Perimeter Def, Steal, Block | Stopping opponent scoring runs | 82 for Gold Clamps Badge |

Physical attributes dictate how effectively your player can utilize their skills. Speed, Acceleration, Strength, Vertical, and Stamina must be leveled alongside skill attributes to ensure your player does not tire out before the fourth quarter.

| Physical Stat | Target Rating | Primary Benefit |
| Speed | 80+ | Fast break transition and perimeter defense |
| Strength | 70+ | Resisting blow-bys and securing box-outs |
| Stamina | 90+ | Maintaining performance late in games |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
Do not neglect Stamina. Low stamina reduces your shot meter size and makes your player sluggish, rendering high shooting ratings useless during long possessions.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Define the Physical Profile">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Distribute Initial Attributes">

  <Step num="3" title="Select Starting Badges">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Strategies and Badge Setup">

Succeeding on the court requires more than just high attributes. You must understand positioning, shot timing, and how to utilize your badges effectively during competitive play.

| Badge Name | Category | Primary Effect | Recommended Tier |
| Limitless Range | Shooting | Extends effective shooting distance | Silver |
| Quick First Step | Playmaking | Boosts acceleration out of size-ups | Gold |
| Anchor | Defense | Enhances rim protection and shot contests | Silver |
| Posterizer | Finishing | Increases success rate of contact dunks | Gold |

Focus on playing smart defense by keeping your player between the ball handler and the basket. Avoid reaching for steals constantly, as this leads to reach-in fouls and puts your player out of position, giving the opponent an easy path to the basket.

<Callout type="tip" title="Defense Tip">
Use the lateral movement trigger to stay in front of quick guards. Holding the sprint button constantly will cause your player to over-rotate and give up open lanes.
</Callout>
